    What is seasonal auto transport?

    Once thermometers start showing low temperatures and rain becomes a frequent visitor in town, many people start packing their luggage for a seasonal move where they can enjoy the warmth of the sun for the next few months. Others start booking vacations by the sea or plan ski trips in the mountains. This is where seasonal auto transport comes in to save the day. Renting a car for such a long period would be quite expensive and senseless. The best option is to ship your car and save time, gas, and potential wear and tear with professional auto relocation services.

    The alternative to seasonal car shipping would be driving a car yourself. But, doing so would be quite stressful and strenuous. Nobody wants to start their seasonal relocation or vacation with such stress and fatigue.

    There is a simple and affordable car moving solution though…hiring seasonal auto relocation services! Regardless of where you are or where you go, Nexus Auto Transport provides professional seasonal auto shipping solutions at affordable prices. Our auto relocation services are flexible to your needs. Whether it's raining, snowing, or the sun is shining bright, Nexus will be there to ship your car when you need it most.

    And, to make things easier for you, we offer door-to-door car delivery which means you don’t have to pick up your vehicle at a terminal. That means you don’t have to go out of your way to ship your car or pick it up. We’ll come to you.
