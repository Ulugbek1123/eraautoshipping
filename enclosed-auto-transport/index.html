    THE PROS AND CONS
    OF DIFFERENT
    CAR HAULER TRAILERS
    When deciding on the method of auto transport, you have several options available. Some offer more protection and versatility, and others are more efficient and cost-effective.
    ENCLOSED CAR TRAILERS
    OPEN CAR TRAILERS
    Enclosed car hauler trailers are small hauler trailers that can only transport up to 2 or 3 cars at once
    Open car hauler trailers transport cars without exterior covering, accommodating up to 10 vehicles at once depending on size and weight
    Enclosed car hauler trailers offer full protection from the elements, reduce the risk of damage to the exterior from road debris, and security against theft
    The cons of open trailers include no protection from debris or bad weather and less security against theft
    However, enclosed car hauler trailers can take longer to transport, are less fuel efficient, and are more expensive
    Open car trailers are more fuel efficient, faster, more affordable, and offer more transport options
    HIGH-MOUNT TRAILERS
    STINGER TRAILERS
    High-mount trailers are a traditional trailer style that uses a 5th wheel that connects to the trailer low to the ground
    A stinger trailer uses combined trailer and tractor head racks, allowing trailers to transport more cars per load
    However, high-mount trucks have more versatility than stingers, allowing them to transport a variety of trailer types
    The advantage of car haulers that use stingers is a better utilization of space on a double-decker car hauling trailer
    Single, Two, Three, and Four Car Hauler Trailers
    Depending on hauling needs, trailers can come in single, two, three, or four-car options.

    When is it advisable to use Enclosed Car Hauler Services?

    As mentioned before

    open car carriers are the most popular automobile shipping option. However, in some instances, the best solution is enclosed auto transport, This is especially true if your vehicle is exceptionally fragile, and a minor dent or ding could end up costing hundreds if not thousands of dollars.

    Exclusive and highly expensive cars as well as racing vehicles

    are typically shipped in enclosed car carriers. Car owners that need to ship exotic, classic, custom, or high-end vehicles should take full advantage of the security and protection which sealed car carriers provide.

    The price is often higher

    than standard open carrier auto transport, but the advantages far surpass the cost. If you consider both the amount of money invested and the time and effort put into a classic or custom vehicle, you can rest easy knowing an enclosed automobile carrier is a sound investment.
