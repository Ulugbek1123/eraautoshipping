    How it works

    Schedule a vehicle shipment with Nexus

    We pick up your car and the vehicle transport journey begins.

    Our car hauling service delivers your vehicle to its destination.

    Step 1
    Schedule a vehicle
    shipment with Nexus
    The best car transport service for you should make scheduling shipments simple. That's what we do.
    ZIP CODES
    Enter your car pickup and car delivery zip codes
    VEHICLE INFO
    Select your vehicle type and size
    OPEN OR ENCLOSED
    Choose between open or enclosed auto transport
    224-218-2949
    Step 2
    We pick up your vehicle and the
    vehicle transport journey begins.
    Our expert Nexus auto transport coordinators are standing by to book your spot with one of our experienced, safe vehicle transporters.
    GET READY
    Prep your vehicle for car hauling by cleaning it inside and out.
    BE PREPARED
    Remove all personal items, disable alarms, and ensure your car has a quarter to half tank of gas.
    TIME TO GO
    A driver dispatched by Nexus will thoroughly inspect your vehicle, and off it goes!
    Step 3
    Our car hauling service
    delivers your vehicle to its destination.
    We’re a vehicle shipping company built for you. You can contact your car transporter at any time for info about your vehicle shipment.
    PAY NOW OR LATER
    You can even pay your vehicle shipment balance upon arrival.
    ONE LAST CHECK
    Your driver will conduct a final vehicle inspection with you.
    YOU’RE DONE
    Just sign off on a Bill of Lading, and you’re done!
